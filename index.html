Why COEDIFY ?

As the name suggests, Coedify is the together upliftment program, here we work with the agenda of upgrading the IT professionals for the higher salary opportunities. we start with the very basics of everything be it programming language, web architecture. Coedify completely train the candidates for a full-stack developer career. Our mentor stands from the top MNCs like Oracle, Google, Amazon with more than 15 years of experience. [We have scheduled mock tests on a weekly basis to test the candidates  and takes real-time mock interviews.](we can cut this section) At Coedify, we recognized the gap between the skills of the candidates and the required skills for the industry. Therefore, bridging the gap and enabling the aspirant to grab their desired job.

About Us

Coedify is a 3 to a 6-month rigorous training program that works for IT professionals to land their dream job in the world's leading tech companies.  One to one mentor is allotted to ensure the interpersonal communication skills of the candidate. As everyone has their own requirements,  a personalized road map is created by experienced mentors who will assess the skills of the applicants and guides them until they get their hands on their dream job.
